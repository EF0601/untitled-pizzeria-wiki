General

The auto-clicker was added in version 0.4.0-beta, which prompted the release of 1.0.0. It confirms that the player is playing the game, as the developers wanted to make sure people enjoy the game and not afk it.

The afk detector consists of two timers and a modal. The modal is activated after the first time finishes (10 minutes), which activates the second timer for 3 minutes, and when that timer ends the page is reloaded, quitting the game.
