General

The auto-clicker was added in version 0.3.0-beta. It performs the clicking motion of a player four times per second. It can focus on one factory button at a time. The button with the auto-clicker focused on will have a black outline.

Activation

The auto-clicker can be activated by right-clicking a factory button. Disable all active auto-clickers by clicking the "clear auto-clicker" button.
